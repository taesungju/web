CS Unplugged : Computer Science without a computer

CS Unplugged is a collection of free teaching material that teaches Computer Science through engaging games and puzzles that use cards, string, crayons and lots of running around.

About CS Unplugged,
Professor Tim Bell of New Zealand has developed the ability to learn the principles of computer science in a computer-free environment.
Through teaching and learning, he developed the ability to learn the principles of computers through play and apply computer principles to problem solving.
It originally developed this so that young students could dive head-first into Computer Science, experiencing the kinds of questions and challenges that computer scientists experience, but without having to learn programming first.
